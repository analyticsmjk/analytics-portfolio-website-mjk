Data warehouse vs Operations system

The Data Warehouse Toolkit : The Complete Guide to Dimensional Modeling by Ralph Kimball, Margy Ross

Data warehouse has a very different user requirement when compared to the requirements of an operational system like the database system that records all sold items in realtime.

The most important is datawarehouse is to answer questions that will support business decision making where as operational systems are systems of record. 

This oftens leads to very different technical requiremnts such as operational systems being designed for fast writes and data warehouses being designed for fast reads.
